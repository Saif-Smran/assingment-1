The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh stands at the threshold of a new era, driven by the dreams and determination of its people. As we embrace technological advancements, sustainable development, and inclusive growth, our nation becomes a beacon of possibility in the region. Together, we are shaping a future where education empowers minds, innovation transforms industries, and unity bridges divides. Our journey is one of resilience, hope, and collective progress, ensuring that no one is left behind in this transformation. Let us work hand in hand to build a Bangladesh 2.0—modern, prosperous, and equitable for all"

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
